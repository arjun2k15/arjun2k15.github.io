Arjun Khurana

CSE | University of Michigan

I am a graduate student studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan. I have been fortunate to work closely with professors Jason Mars and Lingjia Tang as a member of Clarity-Lab. My research interests lie in the intersection of computer architecture and software systems. I've worked on projects ranging from implementing hardware accelerators to building different microservices. Currently, I am dabbling in the applications and intricacies of distributed systems.

Outside of school, I enjoy pick-up sports, strategy games, and non-fiction. I also love listening to all kinds of music.
